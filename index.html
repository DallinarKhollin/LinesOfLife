About Me

Like many other Ukrainians, I had to leave the country because of the war outbreak. Now I am in the U.S., however I am still haunted by the fears because of my experience. But I have one salvation, which helps me to calm my nerves and bring my thoughts back to normal - it's painting! And because I really want to support my country - I offer not indifferent people to buy this paintings from me. All money will be sent to the Ukrainian fund "Come Back Alive" with confirmation for you on my part. You can also choose a short wish to the payment, which I will use when transferring!

In the tab "My paintings" you can select a painting you like and contact me via the e-mail indicated on the site. Thank you for your support of Ukraine!
